EventIntervals.chunk — Method
chunk(int::Interval{E, 1}, chunk_len::E [, exact::Bool = false]) where E

Break an interval, int into smaller chunks of length chunk_len. If exact is true, then any remainder of int will be dropped. Otherwise, the last chunk may not be of measure chunk_len.
